Maintaining a healthy lifestyle

Maintaining a healthy lifestyle has many benefits. It helps to reduce stress, anxiety, and depression. It boosts mental health and helps to live a happy, productive life.

Below is a list of a few ways to maintain a healthy lifestyle.

  1. Measure and watch your weight
  2. Limit unhealthy foods and eat healthy meals
  3. Take Multivitamin supplements
  4. Drink water, stay hydrated, and limit sugared beverages
  5. Exercise regularly and be physically active
  6. Get enough good sleep
  7. Reduce sitting and screen time
  8. Go easy on alcohol

Below is a small list of healthy foods.

  • Vegetables
  • Fruits
  • Whole grain foods
  • Protein foods "lean meat, beans, nuts, eggs"
  • Seafood
